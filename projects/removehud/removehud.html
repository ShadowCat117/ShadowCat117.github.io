RemoveHUD

RemoveHUD is a Minecraft mod for Fabric that allows users to customise the layout of their HUD. It features the ability to toggle or simply change the position of where elements are rendered.

My contributions to the mod were porting it to 1.20.2 and porting it to 1.21. In the 1.21 port I also added a few new options with offsets for the exp bar, hotbar, overlay message and held item tooltip.
